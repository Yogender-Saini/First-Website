Introduction

Our own planet, Earth, is a world unto itself. Earth, the third planet from the sun, is the only place in the known cosmos where the presence of life has been established. With a 3,959-mile radius, Earth is the fifth-largest planet in our solar system and the only one whose surface is known to have liquid water.
